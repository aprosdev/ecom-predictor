Follow these steps to upload product images:

  1. Choose your image.
  2. Format and name your image.
  3. Click Browse below to find the image (or zip) file on your computer and click Submit to upload the file.
  4. Review the status of your upload.

Important: Because it may take up to one or two business days for Quality Assurance to review your images, check the Review Status Page again after that time to verify the status of your image uploads.
